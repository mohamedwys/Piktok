## 3. Personal Data We Collect

**Account & profile**

**Listings & content**
- Photos and descriptions of products you list

**Location**
- Approximate location used for the location filter, if you grant permission

**Payment data**
- We do not store payment card details. Payments are processed by Stripe, Inc. We

**Device & usage**

- Crash logs and error reports

**Analytics**
- Aggregate counts of product views. We do not build cross-site advertising profiles.

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. After account
deletion (via the in-app "Delete account" option), we permanently delete your
personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g.,
financial records — retained for 5 years per UAE law).

## 7. Security

encryption at rest, role-based access control, and row-level security on all user
data tables.

No system is perfectly secure. Use a strong password and notify us at
Support@app-mony.com if you suspect unauthorized access.
