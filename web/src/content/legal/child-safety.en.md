# Child Safety Standards

_Last updated: [DATE]_

Mony has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content or
behavior that exploits, endangers, or sexualizes minors. This page describes our
standards and procedures, in compliance with applicable laws and platform
requirements (including Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy and Apple's
App Store Review Guidelines).

## Our Standards

We prohibit:

- Any content depicting child sexual abuse, exploitation, or grooming (CSAE)
- Solicitation of minors for sexual or exploitative purposes
- Sharing personal information of minors without consent
- Any imagery or text that sexualizes minors, real or simulated

## Age Requirement

Users must be at least 16 years old to register. We do not knowingly permit users
under 16 on the platform.

## Detection & Moderation

We use a combination of:

- Reactive moderation based on user reports
- Proactive automated screening of uploaded media for known CSAM signatures, where
  technically feasible
- Manual review of flagged content
- Cooperation with industry hash-sharing initiatives

## How to Report

If you encounter content or behavior on Mony that endangers or exploits a child,
report it immediately.

**In-app:** Use the "Report" option on any listing, comment, or profile and select
"Child safety / CSAE" as the reason.

**Email:** Send details to Support@app-mony.com with "CHILD SAFETY" in the subject
line. We treat these reports as urgent and review them within 24 hours.

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact local law enforcement first.

## Resources

- National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) — CyberTipline:
  https://report.cybertip.org
- Internet Watch Foundation (IWF): https://www.iwf.org.uk
- UAE Child Protection Hotline (Wadeema): 800 700

## Our Response Process

When we receive a credible report:

1. We remove the content within 24 hours of confirmation.
2. We suspend the account pending investigation.
3. Where required by law, we report to NCMEC and/or UAE authorities.
4. We preserve evidence as required by applicable law.

We comply with UAE Federal Law No. 3 of 2016 on Child Rights ("Wadeema's Law") and
Federal Decree-Law No. 34 of 2021 on Combating Rumors and Cybercrimes.

For all child safety inquiries, including law enforcement requests, contact:

We aim to respond within 24 hours.
